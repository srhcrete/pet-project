My Pet Tarantula, Boris

All about my pet, Boris the spider!

I have an unusual pet. Her name is Boris and she is a Tarantula. She is not fluffy and cuddly like most pets, but she's cute to me!

Reasons Tarantulas make great pets

Here are some reasons Tarantulas make excellent pets:

  1. They are clean
  2. They don't make any noise
  3. They are inexpensive
  4. They are fun to watch!

Fun facts about Tarantulas

Here are some fun facts about Tarantulas and Boris the spider in particular!

  • Female Tarantula's can live up to 30-40 years in captivity.
    (Boris is already 12 years old!)
  • Most tarantulas are effectively blind, their primary sense is touch.
  • Tarantulas molt there skin every 1-2 years.
